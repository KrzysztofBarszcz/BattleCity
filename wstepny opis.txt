The project will be a clone of the popular Nintendo game - Battle City. In this game, the player has to destroy enemy tanks and protect his base from them. There are some types of enemies: slow but very resistant with strong cannon, fast but vulnerable, balanced. Game offer various bonuses like better cannon, additional lifes, etc.


Specification:
UI implemented in WPF
level editor
two players mode
highscore table
many levels (at least more than one :))
internet game (maybe)
